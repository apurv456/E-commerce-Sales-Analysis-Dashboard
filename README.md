Project context: Interactive Ecommerce Sales Dashboard built in Power BI for cross-platform sales analysis.

Project details
  - Type: Data Visualization & Business Intelligence
  - Tools: Power BI, DAX, Excel
  - Domain: E-commerce Analytics

Project objective
  - Analyze sales performance across platforms, cities, categories, and products
  - Track KPIs, identify trends, and enable data-driven decisions with dynamic filters and visuals

Key features & insights
  - KPI cards: Total Sales, Total Orders, Total Quantity, Average Order Value (AOV)
  - Monthly sales trends to reveal seasonality and revenue fluctuations
  - Platform performance comparison via donut charts (Amazon, Jumia, Souq)
  - Top 10 products; sales by category and by city
  - Explore relationship between product ratings and customer reviews
  - Interactive slicers: Date, Platform, City for dynamic analysis

Technical highlights
  - Optimized DAX measures for sales, orders, quantities, and averages
  - Adherence to dashboard design principles: alignment, formatting, consistency
  - Robust data modeling and aggregation for accurate reporting
  - Readability enhancements with unit scaling (K, M) and reduced decimal precision
  - Executive-ready visuals and tooltips for quick insights
